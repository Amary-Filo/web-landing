Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Sintair ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you visit our website and use our contact forms. We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Data We Collect

We collect personal data that you voluntarily provide to us when filling out forms on our website:

  • Identity & Contact Data: Your Telegram handle, Email address, and Country.
  • Project Data: Information about your project type (e.g., Proof of Concept, MVP) and your project brief.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and usage data collected automatically via analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To contact you regarding your submitted brief and discuss potential collaboration.
  • To provide an approximate estimation of project cost and timeline.
  • To analyze website traffic and improve user experience.

3. Data Transfer and Third Parties

By submitting your information, you consent to its processing by our team. We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. However, we use the following third-party service providers who may process technical data:

  • Google Analytics: For website traffic analysis. Google Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel: For website hosting and performance analytics. Vercel Privacy Policy.
